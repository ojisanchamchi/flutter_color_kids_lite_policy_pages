ColorKids Lite Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 19, 2025

Overview

ColorKids Lite is a tap-to-fill coloring application designed specifically for children under 13 years old. The app operates entirely offline and provides a safe, ad-free environment for young children to express their creativity through digital coloring activities.

Information Collection

ColorKids Lite does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from users. All coloring activities and creations remain solely on your device. We do not collect names, ages, locations, or any other personal data from users.

Data Storage and Security

The app does not use remote servers or cloud storage. All colored pictures and saved artwork stay local to your device's secure storage. Users have full control over any saved content on their device.

Internet and Network Usage

ColorKids Lite does not require an internet connection and does not establish network communications. The app works completely offline, ensuring user privacy and safety. No data ever leaves your device through ColorKids Lite.

Advertising and Analytics

ColorKids Lite is completely ad-free and does not integrate any analytics or third-party tracking systems. We do not monitor user activity or collect usage data. The app provides a safe, distraction-free coloring experience.

Permissions

ColorKids Lite may request permission to save colored pictures to your device's photo gallery (if you choose to save artwork). This permission is used strictly for saving your creations and is never used to access or transmit existing photos or personal data.

Children's Privacy Protection (COPPA Compliance)

ColorKids Lite is designed specifically for children under 13 years old and fully complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not collect, use, or disclose any personal information from children under 13. The app operates completely offline with no data collection, ensuring maximum privacy protection for young users.

Parents can be confident that their children's privacy is fully protected while using ColorKids Lite. No personal information, usage data, or any other data is collected or transmitted from the app.

Parental Guidance

ColorKids Lite is designed for children under 13 and we encourage parental supervision during app usage. Parents should review this privacy policy and the app's features before allowing their children to use the application. The app requires no internet connection and collects no data, making it a safe choice for young children.

Google Play Policy Compliance

We are committed to complying with all Google Play policies, including those specific to apps targeting children under 13. ColorKids Lite does not include any features for matchmaking, dating, or real money gambling/games/contests. Our application is designed to be a safe and creative space specifically for young children, and we have implemented the necessary measures in the Google Play Console to ensure that our app is correctly categorized for children under 13 and does not offer age-restricted content.

Changes to This Policy

If the functionality of ColorKids Lite changes in a way that affects your privacy, this policy will be updated and a new revision date will be shown at the top of the page.

Contact

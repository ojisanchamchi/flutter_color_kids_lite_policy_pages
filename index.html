ColorKids Lite Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 14, 2025

Overview

ColorKids Lite is a mobile coloring application designed for children to enjoy creative coloring activities. The app operates entirely offline and provides a safe, ad-free environment for kids to express their creativity through digital coloring.

Information Collection

ColorKids Lite does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from users. All coloring activities and creations remain solely on your device. We do not collect names, ages, locations, or any other personal data from children or parents.

Data Storage and Security

The app does not use remote servers or cloud storage. All colored pictures and saved artwork stay local to your device's secure storage. Parents have full control over any saved content on their device.

Internet and Network Usage

ColorKids Lite does not require an internet connection and does not establish network communications. The app works completely offline, ensuring your child's privacy and safety. No data ever leaves your device through ColorKids Lite.

Advertising and Analytics

ColorKids Lite is completely ad-free and does not integrate any analytics or third-party tracking systems. We do not monitor your child's activity or collect usage data. The app provides a safe, distraction-free coloring experience.

Permissions

ColorKids Lite may request permission to save colored pictures to your device's photo gallery (if you choose to save artwork). This permission is used strictly for saving your child's creations and is never used to access or transmit existing photos or personal data.

Children's Privacy

ColorKids Lite is specifically designed for children and fully complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and other applicable child privacy laws. We do not collect any personal information from children under 13 or users of any age. The app provides a safe, private environment for creative play.

Google Play Policy Compliance

We are committed to complying with all Google Play policies. In line with Google's policy updates announced on October 30, 2025, we confirm that ColorKids Lite does not include any features for matchmaking, dating, or real money gambling/games/contests. Our application is designed to be a safe and creative space for children, and we have implemented the necessary measures in the Google Play Console to ensure that our app is correctly targeted and does not offer age-restricted content.

Changes to This Policy

If the functionality of ColorKids Lite changes in a way that affects your privacy, this policy will be updated and a new revision date will be shown at the top of the page.

Contact

If you have any questions or feedback about this policy or the app, please contact the ColorKids Lite team at ojisancancode@gmail.com.
